Our Partners

Who are in extremely love with eco friendly system.

Impact Royal Integrated Services

Impact Royal Integrated Services, LLC is a multinational organization with offices in the U.S. and Africa led by President and CEO, Moses Atamata. As a vital source of services in various sectors, Impact Royal Integrated Services focuses on positively impacting the goals and objectives of our clients.

Pacifico Energy Group

Pacifico Energy is a privately held, utility-scale renewable power company specializing in development of solar photovoltaic and offshore wind projects, with offices in Japan, Vietnam, Korea, and the United States. Pacifico has over 1 GW of projects either operational or under construction.

Pacifico Desarrollos Energeticos

PDE, is a Mexican-based global business consortium of partners with diversified interests in key sectors like Global Resources, Energy, Infrastructure, Technology, Investment and Country Advisory. They bring together the strong expertise of our partners and team, strong intellectual capital, global connectivity and proven track record to deliver results.

ACS Energy Solutions

ACS Energy Solutions, Inc is an EPC Managing General Contractor with 130 combined experience and with their assistance and expertise we can quickly pull together a large-scale construction project to secure engineering, procurement and construction.

WiWason Incorporated

Wiwason Link(s) is an international freight-forwarding company headquartered in Crestwood, Illinois. They network with licensed customs agents in Nigeria and we are an authorized indirect air carrier (IAC) in the United States. With partners across the globe, our reach covers Africa, Asia, Europe and Americas, coordinated through an ultramodern information technology network.

Strategic Energy Marketing

Strategic Energy Marketing is a petroleum marketer of Middle Eastern, Russian Federation, Nigerian Light crude oil and Colombian crude oil streams to the U.S., European and Asian markets with direct supplier agreements in place globally.
